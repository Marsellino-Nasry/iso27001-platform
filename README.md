# ISO 27001 Ultimate Platform

## ISO/IEC 27001:2022 ISMS Audit & Compliance Platform

A professional, audit-ready **ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Information Security Management System (ISMS) Platform** designed to support **risk-based information security management, internal & external audits, certification readiness, and regulatory compliance**.

The platform provides a structured, standardized, and auditor-focused approach for managing **Annex A controls, Risk Register, Statement of Applicability (SoA), audit checklists, and management reporting**, with built-in **PDF and Excel export capabilities**.

---

## Purpose & Scope

This platform is designed to:

- Support **ISO/IEC 27001:2022 implementation, maintenance, and certification readiness**
- Enable **internal and external ISMS audits**
- Facilitate **gap analysis and remediation tracking**
- Provide **auditor-ready documentation and evidence mapping**
- Act as a **professional consulting, assessment, and demonstration tool**

The platform aligns with:
- ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Clauses
- ISO/IEC 27002:2022 controls
- Annex A (93 controls)
- Risk-based audit and compliance methodologies

---

## Core Capabilities

### Annex A Control Management
- Full coverage of **ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Annex A (93 controls)**
- Control objectives, guidance, implementation steps, and audit evidence references
- Control implementation status tracking:
  - Not Implemented
  - In Progress
  - Implemented
  - Not Applicable (N/A)

---

### Statement of Applicability (SoA)
- Dynamic generation of **Statement of Applicability**
- Applicability and justification per Annex A control
- Automatic calculation of control coverage and compliance percentage
- **SoA export in PDF format**
- Auditor-ready, ISO-aligned structure
